Mobility Overview

Here are some insights and proofs from my mobility journey, including intercultural reflections.

Kobe University – Japon

Kobe University offers the chance to explore cutting-edge technology in an international program. Experiencing life in Japan will broaden my cultural perspective, teaching me flexibility and intercultural communication in a unique setting.

TU Munich – Allemagne

At TU Munich, I aim to deepen my knowledge in electronics and AI while engaging in collaborative research. Living in Munich will challenge me to adapt to a new culture and strengthen my independence and problem-solving skills.

Cranfield University – Royaume-Uni

Through Cranfield’s hands-on engineering projects, I hope to apply my skills in electronics and computing in a practical context. Studying in an English-speaking environment will also help me refine my technical communication and collaborate effectively with international peers.

Dublin City University – Ireland

Studying at Dublin City University will allow me to develop my technical skills in electronics and computing while immersed in a vibrant, English-speaking student community. This experience will enhance both my professional abilities and adaptability in international contexts.
