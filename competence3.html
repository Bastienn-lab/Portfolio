Study of the cyberattack targeting Nuxe in 2023

As part of a school project, I created an awareness video focusing on the recent cyberattack targeting Nuxe, carried out via the LockBit malware. Using advanced tools such as Capcut and Photoshop, I crafted a compelling narrative to describe the attack mechanisms and their impacts.

This work allowed me to dive into the technical details of cyberattacks and develop a deep understanding of defense and prevention strategies in cybersecurity. Each step of the project, from initial research to final production, was essential for refining my skills in visual communication and IT security analysis.

Tools Used

  • Capcut
  • Dall-E/SeaArt
  • Photoshop
  • D-id
  • Listnr
  • HeyGen
