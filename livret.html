Creation of a welcome booklet for future R&T students

During my studies in Networks and Telecommunications, I had the opportunity to create a booklet for first-year students of the BUT Networks and Telecommunications program. The purpose of this project was to provide them with essential information about the curriculum, schedules, as well as practical recommendations regarding dining options.

This work allowed me to improve my skills in visual communication and writing, focusing on creating informative content with a light tone and designing an attractive visual layout.

This experience demonstrated my ability to manage a project from start to finish, from gathering information to the final formatting, while integrating creative solutions to capture the attention of our target audience.

Tools Used

  • Canva
  • SeaArt
  • Dall-E
  • Microsoft Word
