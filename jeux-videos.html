Video games

Next Section →

Survival games

I enjoy survival games like Ark, The Forest, and Minecraft. These games challenge me to gather resources, build shelters, and manage limited supplies while exploring vast worlds. They train creativity, planning, and adaptability in dynamic environments.

First-person shooters

I play FPS games like Valorant and Call of Duty. These games require fast reflexes, teamwork, and strategic thinking under pressure. They sharpen my focus, hand-eye coordination, and decision-making skills in high-stakes scenarios.

Battle royale

I enjoy Battle Royale games like Fortnite, Warzone, and Apex Legends. The fast-paced action and survival competition improve my reflexes, strategy, and adaptability while keeping gameplay exciting and unpredictable.

Strategy games

Strategy games such as League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics help me develop analytical thinking and long-term planning. I enjoy devising tactics, anticipating opponents’ moves, and adapting strategies on the fly, which strengthens problem-solving skills.

Gacha games

I also play gacha games such as Genshin Impact and Honkai Star Rail. These games combine character collection, storytelling, and strategy, offering both creativity and planning challenges. They teach resource management and long-term decision-making.
