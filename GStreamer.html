FluxMedia – Audio / video stream manipulation with GStreamer

As part of SAE 31, I designed several multimedia pipelines using GStreamer to manipulate audio and video streams (playback, visualization, synchronization, processing). We analyzed MP4, WAV, FLAC, and MP3 files and implemented multiplexing, decoding, and real-time visualization solutions.

This project allowed me to develop practical skills in stream processing, Linux command-line usage, and an in-depth understanding of GStreamer plugins (demux, parse, decode, sink).
