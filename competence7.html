Analysis of the coaxial cable

During a study project, I wrote a report and prepared a presentation on the coaxial cable. I explored its history, operating principle, and applications. Using Octave, I analyzed its frequency response based on practical data.

This experience combined theory and practice, deepening my understanding of coaxial cables in telecommunications. I improved my skills in data analysis and scientific communication while applying theoretical concepts to real-world situations. This integrated approach strengthened my ability to process and interpret technical data effectively.

Tools Used

  • Octave
  • Microsoft Word
  • PowerPoint
  • Oscilloscope
